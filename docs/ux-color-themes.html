Making the Choice

All four themes work well for a compliance platform. The question is: what feeling do you want to prioritize?

Trust Blue

Choose if the consultancy wants maximum trust signals and a safe, familiar aesthetic.

Calm Teal ★

Best match for "system has my back" — calming, modern, distinctly separate from risk colors.

Warm Indigo

Choose if you want to emphasize AI innovation and a more contemporary, tech-forward feel.

Deep Navy

Choose if the consultancy wants maximum authority and a serious enterprise positioning.
